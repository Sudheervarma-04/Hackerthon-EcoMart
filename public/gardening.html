Gardening

Sustainable gardening products are designed to minimize environmental impact and promote ecological balance in gardening practices. These products often use natural, organic, and recycled materials, and focus on conserving resources while enhancing garden health.

Key Statistics:

  • Gardens that use organic soil amendments and eco-friendly pest control can reduce chemical runoff by over 90%, significantly decreasing environmental pollution.
  • Sustainable gardening practices can enhance soil organic matter by up to 60%, leading to healthier plant growth and increased resilience against pests and diseases.
  • Sustainable gardening methods, like using hand tools and efficient watering systems, can reduce energy consumption by 20-50%, compared to conventional gardening practices.
