Fashion

Sustainable fashion products are designed and produced with a focus on minimizing environmental impact and promoting social responsibility throughout the entire supply chain.

Key Statistics:

  • Global apparel industry accounts for 10% of global carbon emissions.
  • Approximately 85% of textiles end up in landfills each year.
  • By 2030, global apparel consumption is projected to rise by 63%.
  • Over 60% of clothing ends up in incinerators or landfills within a year of being produced.
