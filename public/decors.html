Decors

Sustainable home decor focuses on eco-friendly, ethically sourced, and durable products that minimize environmental impact. This includes furniture, textiles, and accessories made from renewable, recycled, or upcycled materials.

Key Statistics:

  • The global sustainable home decor market is expected to reach $556.3 billion by 2027, with a CAGR of 7.8% from 2020.
  • Approximately 40% of home decor products are now made from recycled or upcycled materials, helping to reduce waste and promote circular economy principles.
  • Around 55% of consumers prioritize sustainability in their home decor purchases, with a significant increase in demand for products made from natural, non-toxic materials.
